Glyphicons

Glyphicons are sprite form of icons made available free of cost through Boostrap and Glyphicons collaboration. They are simply monochromatic symbols and icons. The usage of glyphicons are very easy in Bootstrap. Normally to show glyphicons in Boostrap page, we would require <i> tag with a special class that starts with 'icon-'. There are two colors, white and dark gray. You can use the glyphicons in any of the components in Bootstrap application.

For much details on how to use glyphicons, see Bootstrap Glyphicons

For more knowledge on Glyphicons, visit Glyphicons
